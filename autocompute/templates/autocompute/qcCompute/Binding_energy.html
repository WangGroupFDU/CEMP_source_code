    1. Writing Molecule Structure

    User can draw the molecular structure for quantum chemistry calculations in the “2D” drawing box on the right. After drawing, click the smiley icon in the upper left corner to convert it to a SMILES format, or click the “3D” button to switch to a 3D view and inspect the molecular structure. The visualization is powered by the JSmol plugin. If you already have a SMILES string prepared, you can skip this step.

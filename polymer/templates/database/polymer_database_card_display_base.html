    Notice:

    Users can explore three curated databases by selecting the cards above. Each database provides access to comprehensive polymer-related data.

    • Experiment Polymer Database: Contains ~10k entries of experimentally/computationally measured polymer data, including physical and chemical properties relevant to materials design.
    • Polymer Calculated Database: Offers 10000 entries of computationally derived polymer-related data focusing on single repeating unit and their properties.
    • Monomer Calculated Database: Provides 10500 entries of monomer-level calculated data, including descriptors derived from quantum chemistry calculation.
